About Retina Disease Detection

What is Retina Disease Detection?

Retina disease detection utilizes AI and deep learning to diagnose retinal conditions early. This advanced technology helps provide fast, accurate results, allowing timely medical intervention. Retinal diseases such as diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and glaucoma can lead to severe vision impairment if undetected. AI-powered systems analyze retinal scans with precision, helping doctors detect abnormalities early and improve treatment outcomes.

Project Tools & Technologies

Our project leverages machine learning, deep learning models, Python, OpenCV, TensorFlow, Flask, and Masking for retinal scan analysis. Additionally, we use modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create an interactive platform. These tools help process vast amounts of medical imaging data efficiently, improving diagnostic accuracy and speed.
